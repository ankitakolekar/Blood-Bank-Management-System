FINANCIAL DONATIONS

The financial services sector is used to deposits, savings and lending; The Blood Bank is taking those concepts and turning them into a nationwide effort to get more people informed about blood donation and, ultimately, increase the giving thereof. By lending time and making a different kind of deposit, the financial services industry will save lives. As is the case with all the donations we receive, financial contributions help empower our Littles (the mentored kids in our program) to realize their potential. With financial donations, you can make a one-time donation or schedule your donations each month. You can also honor a family member , friend, or past Big (mentor) or Little by making out your donation in their name.
